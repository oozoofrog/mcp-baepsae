### Issue 1: Silent failure across all IndigoHID message sends in `InputCommands.swift`

**Location**: `/Volumes/eyedisk/develop/oozoofrog/mcp-baepsae/native/Sources/Commands/InputCommands.swift`, lines 93-94, 97-98, 102-103, 108-109 (handleTouch), and similar patterns throughout
